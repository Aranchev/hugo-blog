Zen Buddhism developed in China during the <mark>Tang Dynasty</mark> by blending <mark>Mahayana</mark> Buddhism, particularly <mark>Yogacara</mark> and <mark>Madhyamaka</mark> with Chinese [Taoist](#main-taoist-virtues) thought.


---

Trilaksana (the three marks of existance):
 - annicca (impermanance)
 - dukha (unsatisfactoriness)
 - annatta (selflesness)
